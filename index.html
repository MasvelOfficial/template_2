who we are

We are a progressive IT company that works on building global Tech businesses in different verticals.

We have developed a powerful, flexible and easily-scalable platform responding to any unique request; and we keep working on its improvement.

The head office is based in France with headquarters in Ukraine (Kyiv) and Israel (Tel-aviv).

The company began to develop first as a startup, but the spirit that gathered in the team from the very beginning inspired the founders with the ease that they decided to maintain and develop until now, which lead the small startup to a successfully, experienced company.
